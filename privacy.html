Privacy Policy

Last updated:

Introduction

Welcome to knoxdl ("we", "us", "our"). We operate the website knoxdl.com (the "Service"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use our Service. By using knoxdl, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our data practices. If you have any questions, contact us at privacy@knoxdl.com.

Information we collect

knoxdl collects minimal data to operate and improve the Service:

  • Usage data: When you visit knoxdl, our hosting provider (Cloudflare) automatically collects standard server log data, including your IP address, browser type, operating system, referring URL, pages visited, and timestamps. This data is collected by Cloudflare and governed by their privacy policy.
  • X post URLs you submit: When you paste a video link into knoxdl, that URL is transmitted to our server-side API in order to retrieve video download links. We do not persistently store these submitted URLs or associate them with your identity.
  • Cookies and analytics: We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors use our site. Google Analytics collects anonymised usage data such as pages visited, session duration, and geographic region. It uses cookies to track sessions. You can opt out via the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.
  • Advertising: We use Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google AdSense may use cookies and web beacons to serve ads based on your interests. For more information, see Google's Privacy Policy.

How we use your information

  • To provide and operate the video downloading Service.
  • To monitor and improve the performance and reliability of the Service.
  • To detect and prevent abuse, fraud, or violations of our Terms of Service.
  • To serve relevant advertisements that fund the free operation of this Service.
  • To comply with legal obligations.

Video content

knoxdl does not host, store, or cache any video content from X (Twitter). When you request a video download, our service retrieves direct streaming URLs from X's content delivery network (CDN) and returns them to your browser. The actual video data is transferred directly from X's servers to your device. We have no control over this content and are not responsible for it.

Cookies

We use the following types of cookies:

  • Essential cookies: Required for the basic operation of the website.
  • Analytics cookies: Set by Google Analytics to help us understand how visitors use the site. These are anonymised and do not identify individual users.
  • Advertising cookies: Set by Google AdSense to serve personalised advertisements.

You can control and delete cookies through your browser settings. Note that disabling cookies may affect your experience on our site.

Third-party services

We use the following third-party services which may collect data independently:

  • Cloudflare — hosting, CDN, and DDoS protection. See Cloudflare's Privacy Policy.
  • Google Analytics — website analytics. See Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Google AdSense — advertising. See Google's Privacy Policy.

Data retention

We do not maintain a database of user download requests or personal data. Server logs collected by Cloudflare are retained according to Cloudflare's standard data retention policies. Analytics data is retained by Google Analytics for 14 months by default.

Your rights (GDPR / EEA users)

If you are located in the European Economic Area, you have the following rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR):

  • Right of access — you may request a copy of any personal data we hold about you.
  • Right to rectification — you may request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Right to erasure — you may request deletion of your personal data.
  • Right to restrict processing — you may request that we limit how we use your data.
  • Right to data portability — you may request your data in a portable format.
  • Right to object — you may object to the processing of your personal data.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at privacy@knoxdl.com. We will respond within 30 days.

Children's privacy

knoxdl is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will indicate the date of the last update at the top of this page. Continued use of knoxdl after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact us

For any privacy-related questions or requests, please email us at privacy@knoxdl.com.
